Departments

Finance and Account

This department is committed to the highest ideals of effective financial service delivery and consistent improvement with responsibilities in line with the hallowed objectives of the scheme. Providing the best financial management services possible to the scheme and its public in a timely, efficient and honest manner.

Departmental Divisions and units

The Finance and Accounts Department has four Divisions

1. Headquarter Accounts:
  • Other Charges
  • Final Accounts
  • Salaries
  • Checking budgets
2. State Office Accounts:
  • Funds
  • Treasury
  • State Accounts
3. Budget Division:
  • Capital Budget
  • Recurrent Budget
4. Ventures And Special Project Accounts:
  • Venture Accounts
  • Special Projects Accounts
Roles of Divisions and Units
  • Advising Management of financial matters and preparing Annual Budgets of the Scheme
  • Managing of funds accruing to the Scheme
  • Establishing accounting systems with appropriate built in control as approved by the Accountant General of the Federation (AGF)
Products and services

Services rendered by this Department include:

  • Payment of allowances to Corp members through TSA
  • Payment of allowances on approvals to staff through TSA
  • Funding of State Secretariats

  • Staff Co-operative
  • Office allocation
  • Pension and post service welfare
  • Staff welfare
  • Industrial relations and labour matters
  • Responsible for all establishment matters in NYSC
  • IT based Personnel records
  • Career Management
  • Posting and Transfers
  • Salary administration
  • Stores managements
I.C.T

The Information and Communication Technology (ICT) Department is a full fledge Department saddled with the responsibilities of providing Information Technology services to the Scheme. It is a pivotal point in which the scheme revolves. The vision is to transform the NYSC into modern technology-driven organization and to bring about exponential increase in productivity and efficiency to all facets of operations of the NYSC through innovative technology tools.

Departmental Divisions and units

Departmental Divisions and Branches: The Department is headed by a director, and has the following Divisions and Branches.

The following are Divisions headed by Deputy Directors:
  • Hardware/Software Development (HSD)
  • Data Management Services (DMS)
The following are Branches headed by Assistant Directors:
  • Hardware Development Support
  • Software Development Support
  • Application Support
  • Data Control
Functions Of ICT Department
  • Enforces standards and procedures pertaining to the administration.
  • Champions the use of ICT in the Scheme, with a view to providing better services in management of Software systems.
  • Participates in the training and the upgrading of technical staff and technicians at all levels. Sets direction and plans for the ICT infrastructure of the Scheme
Corps Certification

The Corps Certification Department is headed by a Director. Geared towards fulfilling the common aspiration of Nigerian graduate youths by deservingly awarding them the Certificates of National Service, Exemption Certificates and Letters of Exclusion and upholding high standard, discipline and promoting uprightness in the issuance of Merited Certificates of National Service and Credible Exemption to graduate youths of Nigeria.

Departmental Divisions and units
  • The Corps Cerification Certification Exemption Verification The Roles played by Divisions under Certification Department are as follows: Certification Production of all NYSC Instruments Issuance of Certificates of National Service to deserving Corps Members. Processing of genuine cases of Correction and Omission cases of Certificates of National Service. Issuance of Letters of confirmation in lieu of Lost Certificate of National Service
Exemption
  • Issuance of Certificates of Exemption to eligible Full time graduates. Issuance of Letters of Exclusion to eligible Part-Time graduates. Processing of confirmed cases of Omission/Correction of Certificates of Exemption and Letters of Exclusion. Issuance of Letters of confirmation in lieu of Lost Certificates of Exemption/Letter of Exclusion.
Procurements

This department Vision and Mission Statements are as follows:

  • Capital Procurement Division
  • Capital Procurement Branch
  • Recurrent Procurement Division
  • Recurrent Procurement Branch
Functions

The Department plays the following roles

  1. Preparation of annual and recurrent expenditure
  2. Procurement Scheduling: Specifications of time-line for acquisition, delivery of goods and services during the fiscal year, or planned period
  3. Competitive Tendering: Operate a vibrant Tender Board with responsible compliance with laid down procedures
  4. Price Intelligence: Ensuring that the quality and quantity supplied conform with specified/accepted competitive pricing
  5. Monitoring and Compliance: ensuring that the quality and quantity supplied conforms to specified/accepted standards
  6. Stock Receipt/Documentation: receiving goods supplied at central stores or designated venues and recording same
  7. Stock Issuance: Release or distribution of goods from the Central Store or designated venues
  8. Technical Standard, Bids and Audit: The Department is responsible for technical expertise on what is required and is open for internal audit
